Behind the Scenes: The Calculations

    Your Estimated Costs

    Max Loan Amount ⓘ
    The lowest of the three calculations above. This is the absolute maximum the lender will finance.
    Base Equity Required ⓘ
    Total Project Cost (Purchase + Rehab) minus the Max Loan Amount. This is your core "skin in the game" for the property itself.
    Est. Loan & Broker Fees ⓘ
    Includes estimated origination, broker, processing, underwriting, and document preparation fees.
    Est. Appraisal Fee ⓘ
    Estimated upfront cost to determine the As-Is and After Repair Value (ARV) of the property.
    Title Costs, Recording, Transfer Tax ⓘ
    An estimate combining state transfer taxes (borrower portion), title insurance premiums, settlement fees, and county recording fees.
