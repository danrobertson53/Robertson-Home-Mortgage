Conventional Programs

Standard Conventional

The standard route. Low down payment options for first-time buyers on single-family homes.

Fannie Mae HomeReady

Low down payment options with reduced MI and a potential $2,500 credit.

Freddie Mac Home Possible

A fantastic conventional alternative allowing for just a 3% down payment.

Government-Backed Programs

Standard FHA

Low down payment FHA options. Flexible credit requirements.

FHA 100% Combo

Zero down payment! Combines standard FHA with a 3.5% second lien.

VA Loan

Zero-down options for eligible military/veterans with no PMI.

USDA (Rural)

Zero-down options for properties located in designated rural areas.

Non-QM & Alternative Income

Bank Statements

Qualify using 12 to 24 months of personal or business bank deposits.

1-Year P&L

Use a Profit & Loss statement prepared by a tax professional.

1099 Income

Qualify using your 1099 earnings instead of a full tax return.

ITIN / Foreign National

Options for borrowers without a standard SSN living in the US.

Fixer-Upper Programs

FHA 203(k) Rehab

Roll the cost of home renovations directly into your FHA purchase mortgage.

Fannie Mae HomeStyle

Finance renovations into a conventional loan. Great for buyers with strong credit.

Second Home Options

Financing options for your vacation or part-time home.

Conventional Second Home

Requires a down payment. Must be a 1-unit property occupied by owner part of the year.

Non-QM (Alt-Doc)

Qualify using 12-months of Bank Statements, 1099s, or a 1-Year P&L instead of standard tax returns.

Fix & Flip Financing

Fix & Flip

Fix & Flip loans are short-term (bridge) debt. Instead of qualifying based on your DTI, lenders look at the After Repair Value (ARV) of the property.

On Your Radar: Lenders cap the loan using three calculations. Your maximum loan is strictly the lowest of the three caps.

Your Estimated Costs

Your loan is limited by the lowest of these three caps:

    Max Loan Amount ⓘThe lowest of the three calculations. This is the absolute maximum the lender will finance.
    Base Equity Required ⓘTotal Project Cost (Purchase + Rehab) minus the Max Loan Amount.
    Est. Loan & Broker Fees ⓘIncludes estimated origination, broker, processing, and underwriting fees.
